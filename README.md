A simple way to buy businesses

# Dependencies:

- [ox target](https://github.com/overextended/ox_target) 

or 

- [qb target](https://github.com/qbcore-framework/qb-target)

- Blips for locations

- Business Name

- Business Price

- The Businesses Job

- The Businesses Grade

- Ped Model

- Sell Back Percentage
